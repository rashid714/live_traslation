## **Specific Corporate Use Cases**

### **1. Global Team Meetings**
**Problem**: Teams across different countries struggle with language barriers
**Our Solution**:
- Real-time meeting translation in 50+ languages
- Automatic meeting minutes with action items
- Searchable archive of all past meetings

**Value**: Replaces $200-$500/hour human interpreters

### **2. Corporate Training & Onboarding**
**Problem**: Training new employees is expensive and time-consuming
**Our Solution**:
- Record training sessions once, use forever
- Automatic translation for international offices
- Interactive Q&A during live sessions
- Knowledge retention when trainers leave

**Value**: Saves $5,000-$20,000 per training program

### **3. Sales & Client Meetings**
**Problem**: Lost opportunities due to miscommunication
**Our Solution**:
- Client conversations become searchable records
- Automatic follow-up task generation
- Team collaboration on client insights
- Multilingual client support

**Value**: Increases sales conversion, improves client retention

### **4. Legal & Compliance**
**Problem**: Inaccurate records lead to legal issues
**Our Solution**:
- Tamper-proof conversation records
- Automatic compliance reporting
- Easy retrieval for audits and legal discovery
- Multi-language contract discussions

**Value**: Prevents million-dollar lawsuits and fines

This approach makes you much more attractive to investors while ensuring you can fulfill your social mission in education. The corporate market becomes the engine that funds your vision to help everyone learn better.
